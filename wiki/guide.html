Guide

Villages

There are new types of villages for almost every biome in the game (not counting child-biomes like Old-Growth-Taiga)

The loot may vary depending on the size and the type of the village

You may find Ruined protals in the centre of the village or Waystones

There are ghost-towns with almost no loot, but they're interesting places to visit


Enchantments

The Modpack adds new enchantments textures, so now you can identify books simply by seeing it

You can change the color of the "blur" effect on the books - to any color you want. Default is yellow-orange-gold now

The Modpack adds new enchantments such as "End Vein" - You can watch an Enderman without making it angry


End Dimension

You have a higher chance being spawned farther from the main end island, so you may bring more block

There are new biomes, structures, such as a "MegaLake" - so you can explore more


Mobs

There are several new mobs, like End-Slime in the end, Ghost in ghost-towns


Structures

Mansion

You can find much bigger mansions than the vanilla ones, with pillager outposts, much better loot, more hostiles, and fun "minigames" (I mean labirinth)
